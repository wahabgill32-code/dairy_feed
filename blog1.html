🐄 Best Feed for Buffalo Milk Production

If you want more milk from your buffalo, diet is the most important factor. Poor feed reduces milk, while balanced nutrition increases production quickly.

🌾 1. Protein Rich Feed

Protein helps in milk formation and animal strength.

🧂 2. Mineral Mix

Minerals improve health, fertility, and milk quality.

🌿 3. Fresh Green Fodder

Green fodder improves digestion and milk flow.

👉 Tip: Always use balanced feed for long-term milk production.
